## Personal Driving School Appointment Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/707253da24f64796968f592238898dcd/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular Users, Instructors, Administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Vehicles
5. Forum
6. Instructors
7. Announcements & News
8. Personal Center
9. Vehicle Favorites
10. Vehicle Messages
11. Vehicle Reservations
12. Instructor Favorites
13. Instructor Messages
14. Instructor Reservations



**Instructor Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Instructor Message Management
6. Instructor Favorites Management
7. Instructor Reservation Management
8. Vehicle Management
9. Vehicle Message Management
10. Vehicle Favorites Management
11. Vehicle Reservation Management
12. Forum Management
13. Announcement & News Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Administrator Management
4. User Management
5. Instructor Management
6. Instructor Message Management
7. Instructor Favorites Management
8. Instructor Reservation Management
9. Vehicle Management
10. Vehicle Message Management
11. Vehicle Favorites Management
12. Vehicle Reservation Management
13. Basic Data Management (Vehicle Type Management, Instructor Type Management, Announcement Type Management)
14. Forum Management
15. Announcement & News Management
16. Banner Management
